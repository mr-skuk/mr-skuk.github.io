About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Physics at MIT Vishwaprayag University, Solapur, India. I earned my Ph.D. in Condensed Matter Physics from IIIT Allahabad in 2023 and worked as a Research Associate at IIT Bombay on novel topological materials.

Research Interests

  • Condensed Matter Physics
  • Magnetism in Thin Films
  • Heusler Alloys
  • Weyl Semi-metals
  • Density Functional Theory
  • Thermoelectric and Thermomagnetic Generators

Personal Interests

Beyond academia, I enjoy engaging in deep discussions, exploring languages and cultures, reading novels, traveling, listening to music, running, and playing badminton. I believe that curiosity and balance fuel creativity in research and life.
